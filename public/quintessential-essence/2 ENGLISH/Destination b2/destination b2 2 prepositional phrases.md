# Prepositional Phrases

## 1. Time and Duration
### 1.1. Specific Moments or Periods
- **at the moment**: currently happening
- **at night**: during the night (adv)
- **at first**: initially (adv)
- **at last**: finally, after a long time (adv)
- **at times**: occasionally (adv)
- **at the end (of)**: at the conclusion of something (prep phr)
- **in the beginning**: at the start (prep phr)
- **in the future**: at a later time (prep phr)
- **in time (for)**: early or soon enough to meet a deadline or schedule
- **on time**: punctual, at the scheduled time
- **for a long time**: over an extended period

### 1.2. Age and Life Stages
- **at the age of**: at a specific age
- **in your teens/twenties/etc**: during a specific decade of life

## 2. Manner and Method
### 2.1. Means of Action
- **by heart**: memorized completely
- **by accident/mistake**: unintentionally (prep phr)
- **by chance**: unexpectedly, without planning
- **on purpose**: deliberately
- **in secret**: done privately or covertly (adv)
- **in public**: done openly where others can see
- **in fact**: in reality, used to clarify or emphasize
- **for fun**: done for enjoyment, not seriously
- **in general**: generally, as a rule

### 2.2. Communication and Transaction
- **by e-mail/phone/letter**: using a specific communication method
- **by credit card/cheque**: using a specific payment method
- **in cash**: paying with physical money
- **in response to**: as a reaction or reply to something

## 3. Place and Location
### 3.1. Specific Positions
- **at the back (of)**: at the rear of something (prep phr)
- **at the top/bottom (of)**: at the highest/lowest part of something (prep phr)
- **in the middle (of)**: in the center of something
- **in the corner (of)**: in the angle formed by two walls or edges (prep phr)
- **in front (of)**: ahead or before something/someone (prep phr)
- **on top (of)**: on the highest surface of something (prep phr)
- **in the distance**: far away, visible but remote (prep phr)
- **on the coast**: located along the shoreline

### 3.2. Travel and Transport
- **by air/sea/bus/car/etc**: using a specific mode of transportation
- **on foot**: walking, rather than using a vehicle
- **on board**: on a ship, plane, or other vehicle

## 4. Condition and Status
### 4.1. Physical or Functional State
- **in good/bad condition**: in a certain state of repair or quality
- **out of order**: not functioning properly
- **in shape**: physically fit (adj)
- **in a mess**: disorganized or untidy (prep phr)

### 4.2. Emotional or Social State
- **in love (with)**: having romantic feelings for someone
- **in tears**: crying (adv)
- **in trouble**: facing difficulties or problems (prep phr)
- **in danger (of)**: at risk of something harmful (prep phr)
- **at risk**: in a vulnerable position (adj)
- **under pressure**: experiencing stress or demands (prep phr)

### 4.3. Financial or Legal Status
- **in debt**: owing money
- **for sale**: available to be purchased
- **for rent**: available to be leased
- **against the law**: illegal

## 5. Quantity and Extent
- **at least**: the minimum amount (adv)
- **at most**: the maximum amount (adv)
- **in total**: altogether, the complete amount (adv)
- **in addition (to)**: as an extra or further thing (prep phr)
- **in comparison to/with**: when compared to something (prep phr)

## 6. Relationships and Interaction
- **in common (with)**: shared with someone or something
- **in contact (with)**: communicating with someone
- **in touch (with)**: maintaining communication with someone
- **by yourself**: alone, without assistance
- **on your own**: independently, without help

## 7. Work and Responsibility
- **in charge (of)**: responsible for something or someone
- **on/off duty**: currently working/not working (e.g., for police, doctors)
- **on strike**: participating in a work stoppage
- **on business**: engaged in work-related activities

## 8. Media and Technology
- **on the Internet**: available or occurring online
- **on the phone**: using a telephone
- **on the radio**: broadcast via radio
- **on the news**: reported in news media
- **on TV**: broadcast on television
- **on CD/DVD/video**: stored or presented on specific media
- **on stage**: performing in a theater or similar setting

## 9. Lifestyle and Trends
- **in fashion/style**: currently popular or trendy (prep phr)
- **out of fashion/style**: no longer popular or trendy (prep phr)
- **on a diet**: following a controlled eating plan (prep phr)

## 10. Opinions and Conclusions
- **in my opinion**: according to my viewpoint
- **in my view**: based on my perspective (prep phr)
- **for instance**: as an example
- **in conclusion**: to summarize or conclude
- **in spite of**: despite (prep phr)

## 11. Schedules and Routines
- **on schedule**: according to the planned timeline
- **on holiday**: taking a vacation
- **in favor (of)**: supporting or approving something
