# UNIT 13: WISHES & UNREAL PAST - THẾ GIỚI ĐIỀU ƯỚC

### 🎯 Tại sao "Past" không phải lúc nào cũng là "quá khứ"?

"If I **were** you..." (tôi không phải là bạn), "I wish I **had** more time" (tôi không có nhiều thời gian), "It's time you **went** home" (bây giờ là lúc)... Đây chính là "Unreal Past" - quá khứ nhưng không phải quá khứ!

---

## 13.1. UNREAL PAST - QUÁ KHỨ "KHÔNG THẬT"

### 🤔 Mở đầu: Khi nào Past Simple không nói về quá khứ?

**Bản chất:** Trong một số cấu trúc, chúng ta dùng **thì quá khứ** để nói về **hiện tại/tương lai** nhưng với ý nghĩa **giả định, không có thật**.

### 📝 CÁC CẤU TRÚC UNREAL PAST:

#### 1. Second Conditional:
- *I'd buy that top if I **had** more cash.*
- **Thực tế:** Tôi không có nhiều tiền (hiện tại)

#### 2. Suppose/What if/Imagine:
- *Suppose I **became** a famous model!*
- **Thực tế:** Tôi không phải người mẫu nổi tiếng

#### 3. Would rather + someone:
- *I'd rather you **didn't come** with me tomorrow.*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Tôi muốn bạn không đến (tương lai)

#### 4. It's (high/about) time:
- *It's (high/about) time that hats **came** back.*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Đã đến lúc nón trở lại thời trang (hiện tại)

#### 5. Polite questions:
- ***Did** you **want** me to send the catalogue?*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Lịch sự hơn "Do you want...?"

#### 6. Wish/If only:
- *If only I **had** something to wear tonight.*
- **Thực tế:** Tôi không có gì để mặc

### 🔄 SO SÁNH REAL vs UNREAL:

| Real (có thể xảy ra) | Unreal (ít có thể xảy ra) |
|---------------------|---------------------------|
| *What if you **are** accepted?* | *What if you **were** accepted?* |
| *Suppose it **rains** tomorrow.* | *Suppose it **rained** tomorrow.* |

### 💡 MẸO GHI NHỚ:
**Past form ≠ Past time** - Quá khứ về hình thức, không phải về thời gian!

## 13.2. WISHES - NHỮNG ĐIỀU ƯỚC

### 🤔 Mở đầu: "I wish" - câu nói của những ước mơ?

**Bản chất:** "Wish" thể hiện **mong muốn** về những điều **không có thật** hoặc **khó thực hiện**.

### 📊 CÁC LOẠI WISHES:

#### 1. Wish về hiện tại/tương lai (Wish + Past Simple/Continuous):
- *I wish I **was studying** history instead of biology!*
- **Thực tế:** Đang học sinh học, không phải lịch sử

- *I wish I **had** more money.*
- **Thực tế:** Không có nhiều tiền

#### 2. Wish về quá khứ (Wish + Past Perfect):
- *If only I**'d known** Burton's was having a sale last week.*
- **Thực tế:** Không biết được tin sale

- *I wish I **had studied** harder.*
- **Thực tế:** Đã không học chăm

#### 3. Wish phàn nàn (Wish + would):
- *If only Henry **would get** a haircut!*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Phàn nàn về việc Henry không cắt tóc

- *I wish you **would stop** smoking.*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Bực bội vì bạn không bỏ thuốc

#### 4. Wish về khả năng (Wish + could):
- *I wish I **could fit** into these jeans.*
- **Thực tế:** Không vừa với quần jeans này

#### 5. Wish trang trọng (Wish + to-infinitive):
- *I wish **to try on** this ballgown.*
- **Ý nghĩa:** Tôi muốn thử chiếc váy này (trang trọng)

### 🎯 PHÂN BIỆT WISH vs HOPE:

| WISH | HOPE |
|------|------|
| **Không thể/khó xảy ra** | **Có thể xảy ra** |
| *I wish I **were** rich.* (không giàu) | *I hope I **will be** rich.* (có thể giàu) |
| *I wish I **could fly**.* (không thể bay) | *I hope the plane **will arrive** on time.* (có thể đúng giờ) |

### 🔥 ĐẶC ĐIỂM QUAN TRỌNG:

#### Không nói "I wish I would":
- ❌ *I wish I would have enough money.*
- ✅ *I wish I had enough money.*
- ✅ *I hope I will have enough money.*

#### If only = I wish (nhấn mạnh hơn):
- *I wish I were taller.* = *If only I were taller!*

### ⚠️ LỖI SAI THƯỜNG GẶP:

1. **Nhầm lẫn wish với hope:**
   - ❌ *I wish I will pass the exam.* (có thể đậu)
   - ✅ *I hope I will pass the exam.*
   - ✅ *I wish I were smarter.* (không thông minh lắm)

2. **Dùng "would" cho bản thân:**
   - ❌ *I wish I would be rich.*
   - ✅ *I wish I were rich.*

3. **Nhầm lẫn thời gian:**
   - ❌ *I wish I studied yesterday.* (muốn nói hối tiếc)
   - ✅ *I wish I had studied yesterday.*

## 13.3. CONTRAST STRUCTURES - CẤU TRÚC TƯƠNG PHẢN

### 🤔 Mở đầu: Có bao nhiêu cách nói "mặc dù"?

**Bản chất:** Tiếng Anh có nhiều cách diễn đạt **sự tương phản** - mỗi cách có sắc thái riêng!

---

## 13.4. ALTHOUGH/THOUGH/EVEN THOUGH

### 📝 CẤU TRÚC:
**Although/Though/Even though + S + V, S + V**

### 🎯 MỨC ĐỘ NHẤN MẠNH:
- **Though:** Thân thiện, không trang trọng
- **Although:** Trang trọng hơn though
- **Even though:** Nhấn mạnh nhất

### 🔥 VỊ TRÍ LINH HOẠT:

#### Đầu câu:
- ***Although** I wore a hat, I got sunburn.*

#### Giữa câu:
- *I got sunburn **although** I wore a hat.*

#### Cuối câu (chỉ "though"):
- *I wore a hat. I got sunburn, **though**.*

### 🎯 VÍ DỤ SO SÁNH:
- *Though it was raining, we went out.* (thân thiện)
- *Although it was raining, we went out.* (trang trọng)
- *Even though it was raining, we went out.* (nhấn mạnh: mưa rất to!)

---

## 13.5. IN SPITE OF/DESPITE

### 📝 CẤU TRÚC:
**In spite of/Despite + noun/V-ing, S + V**

### 🎯 CÁC CÁCH DÙNG:

#### 1. + V-ing:
- ***In spite of** wearing a hat, I got sunburn.*
- ***Despite** wearing a hat, I got sunburn.*

#### 2. + the fact that:
- ***In spite of** the fact that I wore a hat, I got sunburn.*
- ***Despite** the fact that I wore a hat, I got sunburn.*

#### 3. + noun:
- ***In spite of** my hat, I got sunburn.*
- ***Despite** my hat, I got sunburn.*

### 🔥 VỊ TRÍ LINH HOẠT:
- *I got sunburn **in spite of/despite** wearing a hat.*

### 🔄 PERFECT -ING FORM:
- *I got sunburn **despite having worn** a hat.* (đã đeo trước đó)

### ⚠️ LỖI SAI THƯỜNG GẶP:

1. **Sai chủ ngữ với V-ing:**
   - ❌ *My sunburn was terrible despite wearing a hat.* (= cái sunburn đeo mũ?)
   - ✅ *I had terrible sunburn despite wearing a hat.*

2. **Nhầm lẫn cấu trúc:**
   - ❌ *Despite I wore a hat, I got sunburn.*
   - ✅ *Despite wearing a hat, I got sunburn.*
   - ✅ *Despite the fact that I wore a hat, I got sunburn.*

---

## 13.6. HOWEVER/NEVERTHELESS

### 🤔 Mở đầu: Cách nói "tuy nhiên" trang trọng?

**Bản chất:** 
- **However:** Trang trọng
- **Nevertheless:** Rất trang trọng

### 📝 VỊ TRÍ LINH HOẠT:

#### Đầu câu:
- *The fashion show was expensive. **However/Nevertheless**, it made a profit.*

#### Cuối câu:
- *The show was expensive, it made a profit, **however/nevertheless**.*

#### Giữa câu:
- *The show was expensive, it did, **however/nevertheless**, make a profit.*

### 💡 MẸO GHI NHỚ:
- **However/Nevertheless:** Thường có dấu phẩy xung quanh
- Dùng trong văn viết trang trọng

---

## 13.7. WHILE/WHEREAS

### 🤔 Mở đầu: Cách so sánh hai điều trái ngược?

**Bản chất:** Dùng để **so sánh, đối chiếu** hai sự thật khác nhau.

### 📝 CẤU TRÚC:
**While/Whereas + S + V, S + V**

### 🎯 VÍ DỤ:
- ***While** jeans are worn by both men and women, **blouses** are worn only by women.*
- *Blouses are worn only by women, **whereas** jeans are worn by both men and women.*

### 🔄 SO SÁNH CÁC CẤU TRÚC TƯƠNG PHẢN:

| Cấu trúc | Ý nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **Although/Though** | Mặc dù | *Although it rained, we went out.* |
| **In spite of/Despite** | Mặc dù | *Despite the rain, we went out.* |
| **However/Nevertheless** | Tuy nhiên | *It rained. However, we went out.* |
| **While/Whereas** | Trong khi đó | *He likes coffee while she prefers tea.* |

## TỔNG KẾT UNIT 13:

### 🎯 BẢN ĐỒ TƯ DUY WISHES & CONTRAST:

```
WISHES & UNREAL PAST 🌟

Điều ước hiện tại? → WISH + Past Simple
Điều ước quá khứ? → WISH + Past Perfect  
Phàn nàn? → WISH + would
Khả năng? → WISH + could
Trang trọng? → WISH + to infinitive

CONTRAST STRUCTURES 🔄
Mặc dù + clause? → ALTHOUGH/THOUGH/EVEN THOUGH
Mặc dù + noun/V-ing? → IN SPITE OF/DESPITE
Tuy nhiên (trang trọng)? → HOWEVER/NEVERTHELESS
So sánh đối chiếu? → WHILE/WHEREAS
```

### 📊 BẢNG THAM KHẢO NHANH:

| Loại | Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Wish hiện tại** | wish + past simple | *I wish I were rich.* |
| **Wish quá khứ** | wish + past perfect | *I wish I had studied.* |
| **Contrast + clause** | although + S + V | *Although it's raining...* |
| **Contrast + noun** | despite + noun/V-ing | *Despite the rain...* |
| **Formal contrast** | however/nevertheless | *It rained. However,...* |

1. "I wish I will be rich" vs "I wish I were rich" - Cái nào đúng?

## Between Dreams and Reality: My Wishes and Regrets

Life is a delicate dance between the reality we live in and the world we envision for ourselves. **I often wish I had** more hours in the day to chase every dream. **Suppose I became** a celebrated novelist living in a quiet, seaside town? **What if I learned** to paint landscapes? These thoughts are my constant companions. **Although my current life is comfortable and stable**, the pull of a different, unrealized path is a powerful force, creating a tapestry of dreams, regrets, and the constant effort to reconcile the two.

My dreams for the future are painted with the soft hues of the "unreal past." **I wish I were** living a more adventurous life, perhaps in a country where I don't speak the language. **If only I could overcome** my fear of uncertainty, I would pack a single bag and just go. In these moments of daydreaming, I often find myself complaining about my own inertia; **I wish my self-doubt would simply vanish**. My practical side knows it's not that simple. **However**, the dreams persist. A close friend once told me, "It's high time you took your writing seriously," and her words echo in my mind. I know she's right. I would rather I **started** my novel today than put it off for another year.

Of course, looking forward to dreams often involves looking back at regrets. **I wish I had traveled** more in my twenties when I had fewer responsibilities. **If only I had studied** architecture instead of business; my passion for design might have found a professional outlet. These are not bitter regrets, but gentle acknowledgments of paths not taken. It's easy to fall into the trap of wishing for a different past, but it's more productive to learn from it. I once missed a major career opportunity because I was too timid. **I truly wish I had been** braver then. Thinking about it now, I understand the difference between wishing and hoping. I can't say "*I wish I will get another chance like that*," because that implies it's impossible. Instead, **I hope I will get** another chance, because the future is still unwritten.

Navigating the space between my dreams and my current life requires an understanding of contrast. **Even though my job can be demanding, I make time for creative pursuits.** **Despite having a full-time career**, I've managed to write several short stories. This directly counters the common mistake of saying "*Despite I have a career...*"; the structure requires a noun or gerund. My progress is slow; **nevertheless, it is progress**. I find a stark contrast in my energy levels: **while my professional work often leaves me drained, my personal projects invigorate me**. My lifestyle in the city is convenient, **whereas my dream life is slow and rural**. **In spite of the fact that my reality is different from my dream**, I refuse to see them as mutually exclusive.

In the end, life is not about choosing between dreams and reality, but about weaving them together. I've learned that **while it's fruitless to dwell on past regrets**, it is essential to have future aspirations. **I wish I had started this journey of self-discovery earlier, though** I am grateful to be on it now. The person I am today has been shaped by every decision, both the right ones and the ones I regret. A friend once asked me politely, "**Did you want** to submit that story to the journal?" Her use of the past tense made the question feel less like pressure and more like a gentle nudge. That's what our dreams are: gentle nudges from an unreal past, guiding us toward a future we still have the power to create.

## Grammar Features Highlighted:

**Unreal Past:**
* **Second Conditional:** Implied in the wish structures.
* **Suppose/What if:** Used for hypothetical situations (*Suppose I became...*, *What if I learned...*).
* **Would rather + someone + Past Simple:** *I would rather I **started** my novel today...*
* **It's (high/about) time + Past Simple:** *It's high time you **took** your writing seriously.*
* **Polite Questions:** `Did you want...` used to soften a present question.

**Wishes:**
* **Present/Future Wish (Wish + Past Simple):** *I wish I **were** living a more adventurous life.*
* **Past Wish (Wish + Past Perfect):** *I wish I **had traveled** more...*, *If only I **had studied** architecture...*
* **Complaint (Wish + would):** *I wish my self-doubt **would simply vanish**.*
* **Ability (Wish + could):** *If only I **could overcome** my fear...*
* **Wish vs. Hope:** The distinction is clearly explained and demonstrated (*I hope I will get another chance* vs. *I wish I had been braver*).
* **Common Errors Avoided:** The essay correctly uses Past Perfect for past regrets and avoids the "I wish I would..." structure for personal wishes.

**Contrast Structures:**
* **Although/Though/Even though + clause:** *Although my current life is comfortable...*, *Even though my job can be demanding...*, *...I am grateful to be on it now, though*.
* **In spite of/Despite + noun/V-ing/the fact that:** *Despite having a full-time career...*, *In spite of the fact that my reality is different...*
* **However/Nevertheless:** Used formally to connect contrasting sentences (*However, the dreams persist*; *Nevertheless, it is progress*).
* **While/Whereas:** Used to compare two different facts (*while my professional work often leaves me drained, my personal projects invigorate me*; *...convenient, whereas my dream life is slow...*).
* **Common Errors Avoided:** The essay correctly uses a gerund after `Despite` instead of a clause, directly addressing a common mistake.
